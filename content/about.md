Sydney Aikikai is a not for profit and independent Aikikai dojo on Sydney&#8217;s Northern Beaches. Our club is affiliated with ACNA (National Cultural Association of Aikido) created in 1980 by Michel Becart Shihan.

We run classes in Manly and Collaroy.
